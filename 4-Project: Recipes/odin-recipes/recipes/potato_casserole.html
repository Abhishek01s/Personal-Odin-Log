Potato Casserole

Description

This potato casserole is rich and cheesy with a crisp and buttery cornflake topping.

Ingredients

  • 30 ounce frozen hash brown potatoes
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 16 ounce sour cream
  • 10.5 ounce condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cups crushed cornflakes

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  2. Pour hash browns into the prepared dish; set aside. Combine cheese, sour cream, and condensed soup in a large bowl; set aside.
  3. Melt 1/2 cup butter in a large skillet over medium heat; saut√© onion in hot butter for 5 minutes. Add to cheese mixture and mix until combined. Spread over hash browns in the dish. Sprinkle with cornflakes.
  4. Melt remaining 1/2 cup butter in the skillet over medium heat and pour over cornflakes. Bake in the preheated oven for 1 hour.
  5. Enjoy!
